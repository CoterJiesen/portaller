Portaller

Open letter
Subject: "Why Portaller needs you support"

Hi there!

I’m so happy that Portaller has been so useful for you that you found a few minutes to read this request for support!

Portaller today is the service that you can use everywhere in the world to enjoy the most popular entertainment services which originally work only on the US territory and are not available for the rest of the world.

Also, for some countries, this is one of the easiest ways to bypass sites which were blocked by the government. For now the leaders in this category are Russia and China, but who knows who’s next. Psst! Send me the URL if you are thinking that this site must be open for anyone.

When you are reading this text, Portaller is processing around 5 mbps of outgoing traffic and around 500 people are listening to music on Pandora / Sportify and watching series on Netflix / Amazon Video. On all range of devices — from desktop browsers, to smartphones and new Apple TV with apps.

To keep it all working on all kinds of devices I'm investing some resources — time to analyze and understand all those changes that service providers have done, and money to pay a rent for servers and traffic.

If you like Portaller please press the "Donation" button at the top of this page and donate any amount that is comfortable for you. $10, $50, $100 — doesn't matter, I appreciate any of your help.

Thanks!

— Alex, that guy who has made great shows and music available to you.
