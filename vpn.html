Public VPN from Portaller

Are you upset that can't listen Pandora over 3G on a phone when you on a walk or drive a car? We have a solution — set up VPN to Portaller on your phone and listen Pandora even when you away from WiFi. Read how to do this below.

Important note

This VPN service is free and available to anyone. Please keep in mind that:

— Connection over VPN will be more slow than your normal 3G/LTE connection and all your traffic will be sent through Portaller server;
— Currently server accept only 50 users simultaneously. Users above limit will not be able to connect to the server, until someone disconnect;

In order to have a fastest connection and to be honest with other users, don't forget to disconnect from VPN when you do not use it for listening.


Setup iPhone

  • Launch "Settings" and click on "General";
  • Scroll to "VPN" and select "Add VPN Configuration";
  • At the top of window select "PPTP" as type of VPN;
  • Put in this credentials and then click "Save":

    • — Description: Portaller VPN
    — Server: vpn.portaller.com
    — Account: portaller
    — Password: sharedpa$s

  • Return to the main "Settings" windows and turn VPN on.
  • Run Pandora and listen. To disconnect, return to "Settings" and turn VPN off.


    • Setup Android

  • At "Settings" menu navigate to "More networks";
  • Choose "VPN" tab and click "Plus" button to add one;
  • Select PPTP as VPN type, vpn.portaller.com as server;
  • Tab on created VPN, then "Connect" and use this credentials for login:

    • — Username: portaller
    — Password: sharedpa$s

  • Run Pandora and listen. To disconnect, click on VPN again and select "Disconnect".
